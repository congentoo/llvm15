intel-compute-runtime has been bumped to 24.35.30872.45 on the
legacy maintenance branch.

This version includes upstream GCC 15 compatibility fixes that
were previously carried as a separate patch. The patch is no
longer needed and the new ebuild does not apply it.

Other changes: type size reduction, snprintf safety, DRM test
fixes, and compiler warning suppression.

To upgrade:

  emerge --ask dev-libs/intel-compute-runtime::llvm15

The older versions (24.35.30872.32, .36) remain available.
