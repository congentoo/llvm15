Title: LLVM 15 overlay for legacy Intel GPU OpenCL
Author: Ivan Anishchuk <ivan@ivananishchuk.net>
Posted: 2026-04-08
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 2.0
Display-If-Installed: dev-util/intel-graphics-compiler

The llvm15 overlay preserves the LLVM 15 toolchain and the legacy
Intel GPU OpenCL stack that were removed from the Gentoo tree.

This overlay is needed if you have an Intel Gen8, Gen9, or Gen11
GPU (Broadwell through Ice Lake) and use OpenCL. The Intel
Graphics Compiler for these GPUs requires LLVM 15, which is no
longer in the main Gentoo repository.

Included packages:
- Full LLVM 15.0.7 toolchain (llvm, clang, lld, compiler-rt, etc.)
- intel-graphics-compiler:legacy (1.0.17791.18)
- intel-compute-runtime:legacy (24.35.30872.x)
- spirv-llvm-translator:15, opencl-clang:15

The LLVM 15 ebuilds use Gentoo patchsets for compatibility with
GCC 15 and Python 3.13. The Intel packages carry distribution
patches for build fixes.

LLVM 15 core is end-of-life upstream since January 2023. However,
spirv-llvm-translator and opencl-clang continue to receive active
maintenance on their LLVM 15 branches from Khronos and Intel.

For details, see the overlay README.md.
