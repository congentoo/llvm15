Title: spirv-llvm-translator 15.0.22, opencl-clang 15.0.8
Author: Ivan Anishchuk <ivan@ivananishchuk.net>
Posted: 2026-04-08
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 2.0
Display-If-Installed: dev-util/spirv-llvm-translator

spirv-llvm-translator and opencl-clang have been bumped to their
latest LLVM 15 branch releases.

spirv-llvm-translator 15.0.22 (was 15.0.19):
- New SPIR-V extensions: SPV_KHR_fma, SPV_INTEL_device_barrier
- BFloat16 support improvements
- Multiple type correctness fixes

opencl-clang 15.0.8 (was 15.0.5):
- Support for -spirv-ext= flag
- GCC 15 compatibility fixes (upstream)
- Out-of-tree build fixes

Both packages are actively maintained upstream on their LLVM 15
branches. The older versions (15.0.19, 15.0.5) remain available
in the overlay.

To upgrade:

  emerge --ask dev-util/spirv-llvm-translator::llvm15 \
    dev-libs/opencl-clang::llvm15
